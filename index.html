ABOUT ME

Hello friends. My name is Kanha Gupta. I grew up in Katni, MP, a city famous for limestone. I was born on 4th December 2002 to a middle-class family. Since childhood, I have been an average student. I attended Champions School, Katni, for my primary education. Having finished my 5th standard, I decided to switch to a CBSE school, Saint Paul's Senior Secondary School, Katni. My best seven years of education were spent in this school, between 2013 and 2020.

With a score of 76% in class 10th, I made the toughest decision of my life in 2018 - enrolling in the Math-Science stream for high school. Within a month, I realized the journey would not be easy. In 11th standard, my grades weren't as good as I would have liked. Despite this, I worked harder and scored 85.4% in class 12th. It is well known that the Corona virus caused the delay in CBSE board results in 2020. Due to this, colleges' schedules and competitive exams dates were impacted.

On November 2020, I was enrolled in the LNCT Group Of Colleges, RGPV University, Bhopal for Computer Science & Engineering. Covid's situation has prepared us for online classes. The first online college class was conducted on 7th December 2020. My first experience with an online class was that one. For three months, I only saw my friends and teachers through Zoom meetings. All that was unexpected, but also memorable.

During Covid-19 lockdown, I had a lot of spare time. And being a CSE student, I have decided to enhance my coding skills and have begun working towards it. Even though I can't claim to be an expert in coding, I'm confident enough to say that I'm good at it.

Currently, I'm looking for an internship opportunity to expand my knowledge, skills, and experience in a reputable organization. I always aim to reach my creative goals one step at a time and believe in doing everything with a smile.
