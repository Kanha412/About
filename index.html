ABOUT ME

Hello friends, my name is Kanha Gupta. I grew up in Katni, MP, (a city famous for limestone). I was born on December 4th, 2002 to a middle-class family. Since childhood, I was an average student. I attended Champions School in Katni for my primary education and later switched to a CBSE School - Saint Paul's Senior Secondary School, also in Katni, where I spent my best seven years of education between 2013 and 2020.

With a score of 76.2% in 10th standard, I made the difficult decision in 2018 - to enroll in the Math-Science stream for high schooling. Within a month, I realized that the journey ahead would be challenging. Though my grades in 11th standard were not as impressive as I hoped, I worked harder and scored 84% in my 12th standard examinations. It is well known that the Corona virus caused the delay in CBSE board results in 2020. Due to this, colleges' schedules and competitive exam dates were impacted.

On November 2020, I was enrolled in the LNCT Group of Colleges, affiliated with RGPV University in Bhopal, for Computer Science and Engineering. The pandemic situation has prepared me for online classes, and my first experience with one was on December 7th, 2020. For three months, I only saw my friends and teachers through Zoom meetings, which was unexpected but also memorable.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, I had a significant amount of free time. And being a CSE student, I have decided to enhance my coding skills and have begun working towards it. I may not be an expert yet, but I am confident in my abilities.

Currently, I'm looking for an internship opportunity to expand my knowledge, skills, and experience in a reputable organization. I always aim to reach my creative goals one step at a time and believe in doing everything with a smile.
