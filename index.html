ABOUT ME

Hello friends. My name is Kanha Gupta and I'm from Katni, MP, (a city known for limestone). I was born on 4th December 2002, in a middle-class family. From childhood, I was an average student. I have done my primary schooling at Champions School, Katni. After 5th standard, I've decided to move to some CBSE school & went to Saint Paul's Sr. Sec. School, Katni. I have spent my best 7 years at this school, i.e. from 2013 to 2020.

In 2018, after scoring 76% in class 10th, I've made a very tough decision of my life - taking the Maths-Science stream for high schooling. In only one month, I understood that the journey will not be easy for me. My 11th standard grades were not so impressive. But I worked a little harder and however, scored 85.4% in class 12th. In 2020, there was a delay in CBSE board results, and we all know the reason - the corona virus. Because of this, every college schedule and other competitive exams dates were affected.

In November 2020, I have taken admission to Computer Science & Engineering at JNCT College, RGPV Technical University, Bhopal. By seeing the Covid situation, we've prepared ourselves for online classes. Our first online college class was conducted on 7th December 2020. That was the first time, I'm experiencing an online class. For 3 months, I had seen my friends and teachers only in a zoom meeting. All that was an unexpected situations but also a memorable part of my life.

In Covid-19 lockdown, I had a very empty time. And being a CSE student, I have decided to improve my coding skills and started working towards it. I will not say that I'm an expert, but I'm confident enough to say that I'm good.

I always aims to reach my creative goals one step at a time and believes in doing everything with a smile.
